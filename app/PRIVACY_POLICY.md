# Privacy Policy — My Schemes

_Last updated: 2026-07-16_

My Schemes ("the app") helps users browse Indian government schemes offline.
This policy explains what data the app handles.

## Information the app stores on your device

The app is offline-first. The following are stored **only on your device** and
are never sent to us:

- Your saved bookmarks, compare selections and language choice.
- The optional eligibility profile you enter (age, gender, state, income,
  occupation, category) — used solely to screen schemes on-device.
- A downloaded copy of the public schemes database.

We do not operate an account system and do not collect your name, email or
contact details.

## Data downloaded by the app

The app downloads a public schemes database file from our GitHub repository.
This is a normal file download and does not transmit personal data about you.

## Advertising

The app shows ads served by Google AdMob. To provide ads, the Google Mobile
Ads SDK may collect and process:

- Device or other identifiers (e.g. advertising ID).
- App activity and approximate, coarse information used for ad delivery and
  measurement.

In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom and Switzerland, we request
your consent through Google's User Messaging Platform before serving
personalized ads, and you can change your choice at any time from
**Settings → Manage ad privacy**.

Google's handling of this data is governed by the
[Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and
[How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

## Permissions

- **Internet** — to download the schemes database and serve ads.

## Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children
under 13.

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change accordingly.
