## App name

## Short description (max 80 chars)

## Full description

My Schemes helps you discover Indian government welfare schemes — central and
state — quickly and offline. Browse by category, search by name or ministry,
check which schemes you may be eligible for, bookmark your favourites and
compare schemes side by side, in 11 Indian languages.

Features
• Browse 4,000+ central and state government schemes
• 15 categories: agriculture, women, students, senior citizens, business/MSME,
  health, housing, employment, pension, insurance, scholarships, disability,
  rural development, startup, skill development
• Eligibility checker — answer a few questions to see schemes you may qualify
  for
• Smart filters: central/state, age, gender, income, occupation, SC/ST/OBC
• Full details: objective, benefits, eligibility, documents, how to apply,
  official website, helpline, FAQs
• Powerful search across scheme name, ministry, keywords and benefits
• Offline bookmarks and side-by-side comparison
• 11 languages: English, हिन्दी, தமிழ், తెలుగు, मराठी, বাংলা, ગુજરાતી, ಕನ್ನಡ,
  മലയാളം, ਪੰਜਾਬੀ, ଓଡ଼ିଆ
• Works offline after the first download; refreshes automatically
